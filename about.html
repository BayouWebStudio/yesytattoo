About

⚜️YESY TATTOO⚜️

My name is Yesy G, I’m a Cuban native, currently residing in Houston, Texas where I own my tattoo studio, Blessed Tattoo Studio.

As a child, art in all its fashions has always fascinated me. Since I was 5 years old I started to draw and paint, and at the age of 7 I received my first award in art competitions. At 14 years of age, my studies in art officially began. At the age of 16 I had my first art exhibition and at 18 I received a degree in Fine Arts.

I’ve continued my artwork with passion, which eventually led me into the tattoo industry. I quickly discovered that I can implement my art style in tattoos, which only set my aspirations ablaze.

I’ve been tattooing professionally for the past 6 years. I specialize in black and grey realism as well as color realism, specifically renaissance golden era period and subrealism.

Art is my passion and the reason for my existence.
